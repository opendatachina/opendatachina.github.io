    Co-Building an Open Digital Future

    We are a social enterprise in China applying open principles to shaping and governing the digital future

    Open Governance of Key Digital Assets

    Our first workstream is centered on integrating open principles into the management of vital digital assets, including data. For the past ten years, our efforts have predominantly concentrated on promoting open data practices in China.

    Transparent and Participatory Agenda for Digital Rights

    Our second work stream is dedicated to crafting a transparent and participatory agenda for digital rights. This encompasses initiatives aimed at enhancing public awareness of digital rights issues and bolstering individuals’ abilities to advocate for and protect their own rights. Additionally, we are exploring the development of innovative mechanisms to ensure that citizens’ perspectives are effectively incorporated into the governance of digital technologies.

    Promotion of Open Culture and Open Innovation

    Our third workstream is aimed at fostering an open culture, ensuring that contemporary organizations not only adopt openness but also enhance the general public’s understanding of the advantages and principles of open practices.
